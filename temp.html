We are Safer Together

Daisies are friends that live amongst you; the flower's name even translates to "day's eyes". Safer Together is a project that provides anyone but especially women with a buddy system for navigating isolated or public areas that are normally unsafe or uncomfortable for them to be alone in. Need to walk by a dangerous street or alley late at night by yourself? Need to find a bus buddy that goes to the same school as you? Don't have anyone to go see that awesome band at the concert with? Are you in an abusive realtionship and need someone to help you find a way out. Just moved do a new city not knowing anyone? or anyone to party at the club with? Move to a Have a fellow daisies nearby accompany you!
