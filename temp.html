We are Safer Together

Daisies are friends that live amongt you; the flower's name even translates to "day's eyes". Safer Together is a project that provides women or anyone with a buddy system for navigating isolated or public areas that are normally unsafe or uncomfortable for them to be alone in. Need to walk by a dangerous street or alley late at night by yourself? Need to find a bus buddy that goes to the same school as you? Don't have anyone to go see that awesome band at the concert with, or anyone to party at the club with? Have a fellow daisy nearby accompany you!
